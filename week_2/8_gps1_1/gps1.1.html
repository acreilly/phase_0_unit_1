Pair Programming

About Pair Programming

Pair Programming is when two or more people will work together on coding projects. The driver will take control of the actual coding while the navigator helps with the process by discussing their ideas and goals. They can be interchangable roles.

Benefits

You pick up concepts a lot quicker with a pair and larger projects are done quicker. It helps prepare you for jobs in the real world because that is often how they go about their projects.
